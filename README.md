# Pizza Shop Sales Analytics Dashboard (2025)
This dashboard analyzes a pizza shopâ€™s 2025 sales data using SQL and Excel to uncover trends in customer demand, peak sales periods, and product performance. Designed for business owners, it provides clear insights to support smarter decisions around staffing, inventory, and menu strategy.

---

## Business Questions

* When do peak sales periods occur throughout the day and year?
* Which pizza types and sizes contribute most to revenue?
* Are there seasonal demand patterns that affect sales volume?
* Which menu items underperform relative to their operational cost?

---

## Data & Methodology

* Data Source: Transaction-level pizza sales data for calendar year 2025
* Key Fields: Order date/time, pizza type, quantity sold, price, total sales
* SQL: Data cleaning, aggregation, trend analysis
* Excel: KPI analysis, visualization, and dashboard development

Technical queries are documented separately to keep insights accessible to non-technical stakeholders.

---

## Executive Summary (Key Findings)

* Sales peak during lunch and dinner hours, with evening orders generating the highest revenue.
* A small subset of pizza offerings drives a disproportionate share of total sales.
* Seasonal trends suggest predictable periods of high and low demand.
* Several menu items show consistently low sales contribution, indicating opportunities for optimization.

---

## Insights & Business Impact

* Staffing: Align labor schedules with observed peak demand to improve efficiency.
* Menu Strategy: Promote top-performing pizzas and evaluate underperforming items.
* Inventory: Plan purchasing around predictable demand cycles to reduce waste.
* Revenue Growth: Use sales patterns to inform targeted promotions and bundles.

---

## Dashboard Deliverable

The Excel dashboard visualizes:

* Sales trends over time
* Peak ordering periods
* Revenue by pizza category and type
* Product performance comparisons

The layout is designed for fast interpretation and practical decision-making.

---

## Next Steps

If this analysis were extended further, the following steps could increase its business impact:

* Incorporate customer-level data (e.g., repeat purchases, order size) to improve targeting.
* Analyze promotion and pricing changes to measure their effect on sales.
* Track cost and margin data to move from revenue analysis to profitability insights.
* Automate data refreshes to support ongoing performance monitoring.

---

## Assumptions & Limitations

* Assumes accurate and complete 2025 sales data.
* External factors such as holidays or weather were not explicitly modeled.
* Findings reflect historical trends and should be paired with operational judgment.
